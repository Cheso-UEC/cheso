About DEPARTMENT OF CHEMICAL ENGINEERING.

The Department of Chemical Engineering offers a B.Tech program. With intricate and state of the art lab facilities, a rigorous curriculum and trained by highly qualified and experienced faculty members, it churns out the best chemical engineers in entire world every year. It also offers a variety of elective courses such as Biochemical engineering, Polymer technology, Pharmaceutical engineering, Petroleum Technology, etc.

About ChESo.

Established in 2000, ChESo is a society of the Department of Chemical Engineering, Ujain Engineering College. Being a non-profit organization aimed to bridge the gap between students, industry, and academia. We aim to manifest the very spirit of Chemical Engineering in young students from all corners of India. With the noble intention of advancing India's knovwledge reserves in chemical science and technology, the Institute has grown to become a premier college devoted to education, training, research and industrial collaboration in chemical engineering and chemical technology in entire India & abroad as well.
